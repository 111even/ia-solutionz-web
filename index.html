Product 02 — SPS

School Pickup
Solution

School pickup is often the most stressful part of the school day. SPS brings calm, visibility, and accountability to the process — without disrupting how your school already operates.

🔔
Instant Teacher Notifications Teachers are alerted the moment a parent checks in at the gate.
📋
Automatic Pickup Logs Every pickup is recorded. Missed or late collections are immediately visible.
🪪
QR Card System Parents present a simple QR card at the gate. No app, no account, no friction.
🛡️
Fail-Safe Design If the system is unavailable, pickup continues normally. No child is ever blocked.
🚀
Pilot First, Always Start with one class for a few days. Fully reversible. No obligation to proceed.
